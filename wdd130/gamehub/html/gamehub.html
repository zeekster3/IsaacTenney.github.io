Code And Collaborate With GameHub

The ability to connect with others will make this website self-sufficient with the content it produces. By giving people a way to produce games together in real-time, it increases how fast they can be made. Also, you can seamlessly allow others to see what you are working on and let them make suggestions which may give you new insights for your project.

Interaction:

There is a chatting feature that will allow you to communicate with your friends, this can be used to exchange ideas, discuss problems your facing, or just enjoy talking about what you've accomplished. You can also upload games or programs for others to view and enjoy.
